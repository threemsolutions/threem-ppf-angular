Streamline Your Management with PPF Management System

The PPF Management System provides a robust platform designed to streamline the management of Public Provident Fund (PPF) accounts, clients, and users. With integrated role-based access, the system ensures secure and efficient administration of client information, user roles, and transactions. Features include seamless registration, login, and role management, enabling Super Admins, Admins, and Users to perform tasks based on their respective permissions. The system supports real-time data processing, robust authentication with JWT, and a user-friendly dashboard for easy navigation. Whether managing client accounts or overseeing user permissions, the PPF Management System is your all-in-one solution for handling administrative and financial workflows with ease.
